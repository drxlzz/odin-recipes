Recipe of the Best Hamburger ever

Description:

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling. This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and no tasteless bread crumbs! Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients:

  • 1 and a half pound of lean ground beef
  • a half onion, finely chopped
  • a half cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese
  • 1 ounce envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • a half teaspoon of crushed and dried rosemary
  • salt and papper it to taste

Steps:

  1. First step:
    2. Preheat a grill on high heat.
  2. Second step:
    3. Mix together ground beef, onion, cheese, onion soup mix, egg, garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands to form 4 patties.
  3. Final step:
    4. Cook patties on a preheated grill until well-done, about 5 minutes per side.
